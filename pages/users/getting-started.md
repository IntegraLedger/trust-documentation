# Getting Started

Welcome to Integra! This guide will help you get started with the Trust Platform.

## Creating Your Account

1. Visit [trustwithintegra.com](https://trustwithintegra.com)
2. Click "Get Started" or "Sign Up"
3. Choose your authentication method:
   - Email
   - Google
   - Wallet (for Web3 users)
4. Complete the verification process

## Understanding Your Dashboard

After logging in, you'll see your main dashboard with:

### Navigation Menu
- **Home** - Overview of your documents and recent activity
- **Register** - Upload and register new documents
- **Verify** - Check document authenticity
- **My Documents** - View all your registered documents

### Quick Actions
- Register a new document
- Verify a document
- View recent registrations

## What You Can Do

### Register Documents
Securely register any document on the blockchain to create a permanent, tamper-proof record.

### Verify Documents
Check if a document has been registered and verify its authenticity.

### Manage Documents
View, search, and manage all your registered documents in one place.
